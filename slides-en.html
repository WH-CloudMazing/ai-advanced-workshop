Block 1

The automation checklist

Is this process automatable? Score on 4 criteria:

1. Repetitive Does it happen regularly? Daily, weekly, or with every order?
2. Rule-based Can you describe the steps as clear rules or decisions?
3. Digital Does it involve digital tools, data, or systems?
4. Time-consuming Does it take noticeable time you could spend better?
Score 3 or 4? Strong automation candidate!
5
Block 1

Group scoring exercise

  • Take 3-4 examples from the opener
  • Score each example together using the 4 criteria
  • Discuss as a group: do we agree?
  • Goal: learn to think in terms of “is this automatable?”
Tip: Disagreement is good! It’s about the way of thinking, not the “right” answer.

Block 2

Excalidraw: quick intro

  • Open excalidraw.com via the landing page
  • You only need 3 things: rectangles, arrows, text
  • It’s a thinking tool, not a design tool
  • Ugly is fine! It’s about the process, not the looks
Key message: “If you can draw it, you can automate it.”

Block 5

What is n8n?

  • Open source and self-hostable — your data stays with you
  • Free on your own server, or hosted via n8n.cloud
  • Same concept as Make.com: visual flows with modules and connections
  • Code nodes: write JavaScript/Python when needed
  • Self-hosted = no data leaves your building (relevant for IP)
  • Community nodes for niche integrations
